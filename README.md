### Sales Report  

**Project Objectives**  
1. Developed a customer performance report to evaluate individual buyer contributions and revenue impact.  
2. Conducted an in-depth comparison between market performance and defined sales targets.  

**Purpose**  
Enable businesses to monitor, analyze, and evaluate their sales activities effectively.  

**Key Insights**  
- Identified emerging sales trends and purchasing patterns.  
- Tracked performance against key metrics and KPIs.  
- Highlighted potential markets for expansion and revenue growth.  

**Role of the Reports**  
- Help in determining effective customer discount strategies.  
- Support negotiation planning and decision-making.  
- Identify high-performing markets and expansion opportunities.  

### Finance Report  

**Project Objectives**  
1. Created detailed Profit and Loss (P&L) statements by fiscal year and by month.  
2. Built P&L reports categorized by markets for comparative analysis.  

**Purpose**  
Provide a clear view of financial performance to support strategic and operational decisions.  

**Key Insights**  
- Enabled benchmarking against past financial data and industry standards.  
- Served as a foundation for sharper budgeting and forecasting.  
- Offered a transparent view of profitability across time and markets.  

**Role of the Reports**  
- Align financial planning with broader business objectives.  
- Instill confidence among stakeholders through transparent analytics.
